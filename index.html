World University Rankings for the Year 2022-23

Analysis and Visualisation of the top 1000 univerities in the world. This exploration tackles how the quality of each university affects it's other aspects.

Number of University in each Country

It can be seen that the United States Of America has the most leading number of universities, closely followed by China. While most of the countries have 1 to 10 universities, it can be observed that developing countries such as Brazil and India have around 20 universities.

Top 5 universities since 2012

The figure shows years when atleast one university had a change in its ranking. Since 2013, the top 5 universities has been the same with Harvard university holdings its top position. Further on, there was no change in rankings between 2013 to 2018 and since 2019.

Influence of the top 10 universities

The visualisation proves that the university does not need to be top ranked to have high influence and vise versa. The higher the influence the better, but it's significance is not high for top ranked.

Research output Vs Quality of Publications

For the majority of top universities (within rank 100), the rankings for Quality of Publication and Research output remains almost the same i.e. its research output and quality of publication is almost the same. However, the diffrence between the two is significant for lower ranked universities.

Quality of education Vs quality of faculty for top 10

The quality of Education and Faculty can differ a lot but what seems to have the most significance on university's ranking in the Quality of Education. Some of the uninversities such as California Institute of Technology or Stanford University have a lower Quality of Faculty (compared to education) and yet they remain as the top 10 universities in the world
